# Data Systems: Real-World Reflections

DATA 400: Capstone in Data Analytics

75 min

# What are we doing today?

Today we are doing small-group work as last time.

You will share your 1-2 things in small groups, pick one example together, put a short headline on the board, and present it to the class.

Your example could be something that worked well, something that surprised you, or something that went wrong. Anything that taught you how statistical and machine learning methods work in practice.

# Step 1: Share in your group (5 min)

Each person shares 1-2 things from an internship, research job, campus job, course work, or any data-related work.

# Step 2: Pick one example (8 min)

As a group, choose the example with the clearest connection to DATA 300: Statistical and Machine Learning.

Shape your example:

| | |
|---------|--------|
| **Context** | What were you doing? What kind of data or problem were you working with? |
| **What happened** | What did you notice or learn? (one sentence) |
| **Why it mattered** | Why was this important or interesting? |
| **DATA 300 connection** | What concept showed up here, and what would you tell a classmate about it? |

# What counts as a tie-in?

Examples: model selection, overfitting/underfitting, train/test splits, cross-validation, feature engineering, regularization, evaluation metrics, class imbalance, hyperparameter tuning, interpreting coefficients, assumptions of a model, bias-variance tradeoff, data leakage, uncertainty quantification.

# Step 3: Write on the board (5 min)

One person from your group writes a short title (3-6 words).

Put it under one of these headings: Model Selection / Evaluation, Feature Engineering / Preprocessing, Overfitting / Regularization, Bias / Variance / Uncertainty, Inference / Interpretation, Data Leakage / Validity, Supervised vs. Unsupervised, Other

# Step 4: Present (about 5 min per group)

Walk through your example using the outline. Tell us about your model.

# Step 5: Wrap-up discussion (15 min)

Using the board: Which topics showed up the most? What patterns do we see? What should you build into your modeling workflow now?
